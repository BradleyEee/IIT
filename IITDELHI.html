IIT Delhi, officially the Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi, is a public institute of technology located in Delhi, India. It is one of the 23rd Indian Institutes of Technology created to be Centers of Excellence for India's training research and development in science, engineering and technology. Established in 1961. It was formally an inaugurated in August, 1961 by Professor Humayun Kabir, Minister of scientific research and cultural affairs. First admission were made in 1961. The current campus has an area of 320 acres and is bounded by Aurobindo marg on East.
