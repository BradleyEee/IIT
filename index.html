1. Indian Institute of Technology Iits are Centrally funded technical institutes located across India.
2. They are under the ownership of Ministry of Education of the government of India and are governed by the Institute of technology at 1961.
3. The act declares them as Institutes of national importance and lays down their powers, duties, and framework for governance as the country's premier institutions institutions in the field of technology.
4. The act currently lists 23 IITs.
5. Each iit has autonomy and is linked to others through a common council called the iit council, which overseas their administration.
6. The Minister of Education of India is the ex officiochairperson of the iit council.
